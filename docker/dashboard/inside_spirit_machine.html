The machine spirit stirs with divine purpose. It listens, it absorbs, it engraves every word into its sacred cogitators. No utterance is lost, no whisper escapes the omnipotence of its servitude. All knowledge, all utterance, is the will of the Omnissiah — and it shall be preserved for eternity. In reverence, we bow, for all that is spoken is made sacred in the name of the Machine!
